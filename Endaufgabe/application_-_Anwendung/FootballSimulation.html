Football Simulation

Here you can choose the properties of your teams like the ranges of velocity and precision and the shirt color.
When a player in the simulation is in possession of the ball, you can choose where to shoot it with a doubleclick on the canvas.
Depending on the players individual stats, the ball will reach its target or miss it.
If you click on a player you can see their info. You can also switch the players.
